Moist and Soft Cupcakes

Description

This recipe creates 30 servings of cupcakes and takes 40 minutes to make.

Ingredients

Cupcake:
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 ounce red food color
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Frosting:
  • 8 ounce cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoon sour cream
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 16 ounce confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Mix together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl; set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter in a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy, about 5 minutes. Beat in eggs, one at a time.
  3. Mix in sour cream, milk, food color, and vanilla.
  4. Gradually beat in flour mixture on low speed until just blended; do not over-beat. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into a cupcake comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Cool in the pans on a wire rack for 5 minutes. Remove cupcakes from the pans; cool completely.
  6. While cupcakes are cooling, make frosting: Beat cream cheese, butter, sour cream, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar until smooth.
  7. Spread or pipe frosting onto cooled cupcakes, then enjoy!
